# Naming Examples

## Valid
- `zip1_ui_observability_core.zip`
- `zip2_ui_observability_rules_reporting.zip`
- `zip3_ui_observability_graph_history.zip`
- `zip10_data_pipeline_export_cli.zip`

## Invalid
- `001_ui_observability_core.zip`
- `zip_ui_observability_core.zip`
- `zip1_UI_Observability_core.zip`
- `zip1-ui-observability-core.zip`
- `zip1_ui observability_core.zip`

The installer must parse the numeric portion after `zip` and sort numerically.
